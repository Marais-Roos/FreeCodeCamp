More About Manchester United

  • 1878 - Formed as Newton Heat L&YR Football Club by the Carriage and Wagon department of the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway (LYR) depot at Newton Heath.
  • 1902 - Club changed its name to Manchester United Football Club.
  • 1948 - Won the FA Cup and finished top in the top four during each of the first five seasons after the Second World War.
  • 1958 - Airplane crash resulted in the death of eight of the first team players.
  • 1986 - Sir Alex Ferguson is appointed as manager.
  • 1990 - Manchester United win the FA Cup and begain a period of success that has continued until the present day.
  • 2012/2013 - Sir Alex Ferguson retired as first team manager. He is still a key member of the club as a director of Manchester United FC.
